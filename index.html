What we are doing

Creating a Generation of Youthful Enterprenuers

Our Vision

To be a foundation of empowering the youth with practical life skills they can use to benefit themselves and Uganda at large

Our Mission

To develop youthful programmers designed to improve the social, economic, health and technological welfare in the community

Why The Youth

Empowering the youth is a very foundamental tool for safeguarding the future of humanity. We do believe the youth are the pillars of tomorrow

Our Background

Empowering the youth is a very foundamental tool for safeguarding the future of humanity. It is the youth who take over when the current human representatives hand over office and responsibilities in any given country across the World. It is a very fundamental call to inspire, guide, mentor and equip the youth. The Strategic Youth Employment Network is a registered CBO wCbo/19/3412 in Wakiso District, Uganda with virtues of being a non profit, non Governmental, non racism, non political nor a non religious affiliated organization.

Our Projects


Project 1 : Rights of Women and Girl Child

4th March 2022 to 14th April 2022
Across the World more than 1 million women and girl child suffer and cry in silence due to all forms of toture. These forms of toture include domestic violence among others. You can make an impact into the lives of Women and Girl child by applying for a volunteership opportunity.


Project 2 : Tailoring

1st Jan 2022 to 28th March 2022
This project will equip girl child and women in Wakiso District with Tailoring skills. The tailoring skills will transition them into well established enterprenuers in the clothing area. With proper marketing skills, the sky will be the limit.


Project 3 : Keep Earth Green

1st Feb 2022 to 3rd March 2022
Volunteers will be taken to various communities to sensitize them about the advantages of keeping the earth green. The volunteers will also educate the communities about the dangers of not having a Green eartn.


Project 4 : Welding

1st April 2022 to 18th May 2022
Welding is the art of blending metal to make doors, windows etc. This project will equip both men and women with welding skills. We shall encourage women to apply in large quantities since welding is considered to be an occupation for men only in Uganda.


Project 5 : Computer Skills

23rd May 2022 to 27th June 2022
Computer knowledge is a must in this tech era. This project will empower the youth with everyday basic skills in areas of Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word and Power point. These skills will give them a sloid tech foundation.


Project 6 : Mid Year Summit

1st July 2022 to 3rdth July 2022
The mid year summit or conference will feature various speakers like Mugume Timothy who is one of the founders of the Strategic Youths Employment Network. Speakers from various dimensions and expertise will share indepth knowledge about challenges and opportunities out there.

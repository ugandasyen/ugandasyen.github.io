Our Background

Empowering the youth is a very foundamental tool for safeguarding the future of humanity. It is the youth who take over when the current human representatives hand over office and responsibilities in any given country across the World. It is a very fundamental call to inspire, guide, mentor and equip the youth. The Strategic Youth Employment Network is a registered CBO wCbo/19/3412 in Wakiso District, Uganda with virtues of being a non profit, non Governmental, non racism, non political nor a non religious affiliated organization.

Our Primary Objectives

1. To help youths repulse education programs and opportunities accessible by the network.
2. To promote youth innovations.
3. To support and promote talented youths in sports, drama and create friendship with other communities in co-operate with environmental conserving agents.
4. To create the spirit of nationalism among the youth.
5. To promote public dialogue networking and information exchange among members and the beneficiary community.
6. To empower HIV/AIDS infected and affected persons by mobilizing them to participate in socila, cultural, economic and health empowernment to reduce stigmatization.
7. To set up workshops and programs and rehabilitation facilities venue for drug affected youth for rehabilitation.
8. To fight drug abuse among the youth by calling them into active leisure.

Project 1

- Rights of Women and Girl Child -
Open from 4th March 2022 to 14th April 2022 Across the World more than 1 million women and girl child suffer and cry in silence due to all forms of toture. These forms of toture include domestic violence among others. You can make an impact into the lives of Women and Girl child by applying for a volunteership opportunity.

Project 2

- Keep Earth Green -
Open from 1st Feb 2022 to 3rd March 2022 Volunteers will be taken to various communities to sensitize them about the advantages of keeping the earth green. The volunteers will also educate the communities about the dangers of not having a Green eartn.

Project 3

- Tailoring -
Open from 1st Jan 2022 to 28th March 2022 This project will equip girl child and women in Wakiso District with Tailoring skills. The tailoring skills will transition them into well established enterprenuers in the clothing area. With proper marketing skills, the sky will be the limit.

Project 4

- Welding -
Open from 1st April 2022 to 18th May 2022 Welding is the art of blending metal to make doors, windows etc. This project will equip both men and women with welding skills. We shall encourage women to apply in large quantities since welding is considered to be an occupation for men only in Uganda.

Project 5

- Computer Skills -
Open from 23rd May 2022 to 27th June 2022 Computer knowledge is a must in this tech era. This project will empower the youth with everyday basic skills in areas of Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word and Power point. These skills will give them a sloid tech foundation.

Mid Year Summit


Open from 1st July 2022 to 3rdth July 2022 The mid year summit or conference will feature various speakers like Mugume Timothy who is one of the founders of the Strategic Youths Employment Network. Speakers from various dimensions and expertise will share indepth knowledge about challenges and opportunities out there.
